About

Learn software engineering by working on apps used by thousands of Columbia students! ADI Labs meets once a week for four hours to develop software intended to make life easier at Columbia.

As a member of Labs, you’ll learn to contribute to open source projects and to take your programming beyond problem sets. Paired with one of our student mentors, you’ll learn software engineering techniques used in industry. Interested? Applications are now open.
